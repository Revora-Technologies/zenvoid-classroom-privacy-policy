Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Zen Void Classroom is developed and operated by Revora Technologies. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Zen Void Classroom mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the application, we may collect:

  • Name and email address provided through Google Sign-In.
  • User account information necessary to create and manage classroom accounts.
  • Classroom membership information.
  • Attendance records and classroom activity.
  • Device information required for notifications and application functionality.
  • Subscription and purchase information processed through Google Play and RevenueCat.
  • Diagnostic information used to maintain and improve the service.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Provide classroom management features.
  • Track attendance and participation.
  • Send notifications related to classroom activities.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve application performance and reliability.
  • Manage subscriptions and premium features.
  • Prevent misuse and maintain platform security.

3. Authentication

Users may sign in using Google Sign-In. Authentication is provided through Google and Supabase Authentication services. We only receive information necessary to identify and authenticate users.

4. Database and Storage

User account information, classroom data, attendance records, and related application data are securely stored using Supabase services.

We take reasonable measures to protect stored information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Notifications

Zen Void Classroom uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver classroom notifications, session alerts, and important service updates.

Notification tokens may be stored to enable message delivery.

6. Advertising

The free version of Zen Void Classroom may display advertisements through Google AdMob.

Google and its partners may collect and process information as described in their own privacy policies to provide and improve advertising services.

Users who subscribe to eligible premium plans may receive an ad-free experience.

7. Subscriptions and Purchases

Subscriptions are processed through Google Play Billing. Subscription management, validation, and entitlement services may be provided through RevenueCat.

We do not directly collect or store credit card or payment information.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only with service providers required to operate the application, including:

  • Google (Authentication, Firebase, AdMob, Google Play Billing)
  • Supabase (Authentication, Database Services)
  • RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

9. Data Retention

We retain account and classroom information for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

10. Children's Privacy

Zen Void Classroom is designed for educational use. Schools, teachers, parents, and guardians are responsible for ensuring appropriate use by students in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

11. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect user information. However, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have rights regarding access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal information.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted within the application or on our website.
